Half-Day Trips

Length: 3-4 Hours

Skill Level: Beginner

Cost: $79/Adult | $59/Child

Services Provided:
We provide life jackets, dry bags, snacks, drinks and all equipment. All you need is a swimsuit. We will also provide a safety orientation as well as a shuttle service back to the lodge.

Adventure:
We will take an exciting trip downriver as we raft through some of Salmon River's best rapids (Time Zone, Tight Squeeze, and Black Rock). A great trip fro families and beginners.

Two-Day Trips

Length: 2-3 days

Skill Level: Beginner/Intermediate

Cost: $425/Adult | $325/Child

Services Provided:
We provide life jackets, dry bags, all meals, and camping equipment. Bring your swimsuit, and a small bag of essentials, and we will set up camp for you. We will also provide fishing gear, as well as chairs, towels, and sunscreen.

Adventure:
We will take a trip down Salmon River Canyon. There will be time for hiking, fishing, and of course, rafting. Many animals will be seen on the trip, including sheep, elk, and deer. We will also stop at a natural hot springs for some fun and relaxation. Great trip for families.

Five-Day Trips

Length: 4-5 days

Skill Level: Intermediate

Cost: $1495/Adult | $1095/Child

Services Provided:
We provide life jackets, dry bags, and all equipment. We will also provide a gourmet camp chef to provide all meals. Camping equipment will be provided, including tents, chairs, fishing equipment, lanterns, etc. THe last night of camping will be in a lodge with showers, pool, and hot tub. All you need is swimwear, overnight clothes, and a few toiletries.

Adventure:
We will travel over eighty miles down beautiful canyons, canyons even taller than the Grand Canyon. We will also stop for Rainbow Trout fishing, hiking up to pictographs, and will float down the River of No Return. Great trip for the sceniv admirerer and adventurer.

Kayaking Course

Length: 2 days

Skill Level: Beginner

Cost: $265

Services Provided:
We provide life jackets, all equipment, and lunch.

Adventure:
This is a two-day beginner course where we will teach you the basics of kayaking. We will go go over outfitting a kayak, how to paddle, look for rapidd and exits, and will teach you proper body positioning.
