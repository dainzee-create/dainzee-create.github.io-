5-Day Forecast

Spring is Here

by Jason Chase

The first sighting of ducks on Shoreline Lake have been spotted. It is a sign that spring has sprung and warm weather is approaching. Although we still have a few flurries in store over the next few days, these ducks show that winter is nearly behind us. But don't go an pack away your winter coat just yet. With the coming of the ducks, comes rainy season. So as much as we want to pull out our sunglasses and sandals, we stil need jackets and umbrellas. But don't despair, the sun will come again, and we'll be swimming at the county pool and barbequing at Haven Park before you know it. So for now, let us welcome the ducks, and prepare for the new life that will start to spring forth.
