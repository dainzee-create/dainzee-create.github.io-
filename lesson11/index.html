Small Town Big Hearts

by Dayna Medina

The small town of Preston, Idaho is gathering together to create a big difference in their community. After a severe storm last week, the park was devastated by winds that stripped trees, ripped up plants, and ruined several of the playgound equipment. The Mayor has asked that the community members do what they can to donate. Memmbers of the community have donated plants, money, and time, working together to bring back the treasured park and town square to its former glory.Morris Moss, groundskeeper for the park is overwhelmed by the support from his community, and is truly grateful for their charitable kindness.Through trials, people come together, and this is a fine example of how communities grow through shared love and hard-work.
