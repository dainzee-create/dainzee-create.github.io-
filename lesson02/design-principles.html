White Space & Clean Design

Vogue

Vogue.com

This page uses white space between articles and images as well as in the spacing of the text. The large spaces between each section helps to focus each element and creates nice flow. The simple black and white design is a great example of a clean, yet elegant, look.

Contrast

Jet Blue

JetBlue.com

This page uses color to give contrast. The dark blue contrasts with the white and the orange to give the page a bit of pop. There is also contrast with the sizing of the text and other elements, making the page dynamic.

Visual Hierarchy

Oreo

Oreo.com

This page shows visual hierarchy by using larger elements at the top and smaller on the bottom. Each section has its own hierarchy, making it clear what each section is about. The most important information is largest, and the least important is smallest, giving the page organization.
