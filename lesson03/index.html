Site Purpose

The site's purpose is to provide information on weather conditions in specific regions within Idaho, including: Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven. The site will provide weekly forecasts, as well as current weather conditions with images. We will also provide a storm center, where possible, or current storms will be tracked. The site will be available on all devices, as the website will be responsive, and will provide for the client's indivudual circumstances. The site hopes to be a hub for residents, and visitors, of these cities, and provide honest, accurate, and up-to-date weather predictions.
